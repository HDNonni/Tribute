A Tribute to My Dad

My Story about Eugene C.LaFauci Sr

  • My Dad was the strongest, kindest and most awesome person I have ever had the privledge of knowing.
  • He was tough but fair and I have the utmost respect for him.
  • He was born in Waterbury October 20,1922 to Paul and Rosaria LaFauci.
  • He was one of 12 children and a twin of whom died at birth
  • A hard working family who lived in Waterbury, where work held greater importance than school
  • Eugene proudly served in World War II as a Medic in the army .
  • He married Eleanor on June 2 195something and had 3 children.
  • He worked at Scovill's Oakville Pin Division
  • We grew up on a small farm in Middlebury where we all pitched in in one way or another.
  • Unfortunately he passed on February 2 1980 a few months short of him meeting his first grandchild.
